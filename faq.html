Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know about how Radia works, what it costs, and how we keep your photos private. Still curious? Reach out and we will help.

You take a selfie in good light, and Radia analyzes it in under ten seconds. It reads signals like hydration, texture, clarity, tone, and pores, then turns them into your Glow Score and a routine matched to what your skin needs. You can re-scan anytime to see how things change.
Your photos are yours. They are used only to generate your analysis and are stored in a private storage bucket tied to your account, not shared publicly. We do not sell your data and we never use your photos for advertising. You can delete your photos and your whole account from inside the app. See our Privacy Policy for the full detail.
You can start free: your first scan and basic results cost nothing. Radia Plus unlocks unlimited scans, full progress tracking, and the AI coach for $5.99 per month or $39.99 per year (the annual plan is the best value and includes a 3 day free trial). See the full breakdown on our pricing page.
The Glow Score is a single, easy to read number that summarizes the state of your skin across the signals Radia reads. Instead of juggling a dozen metrics, you get one score to track. As you follow your routine and re-scan, you can watch it climb, which makes progress feel real and motivating.
Radia is designed to give you a clear, consistent read of your skin and is most useful for tracking change over time. Good, even lighting and a clear, makeup free photo give the best results. It is a cosmetic tool, not a clinical instrument, so treat it as helpful guidance rather than a precise medical measurement.
No. Radia provides cosmetic skincare guidance only. It is not a medical device and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you have a skin concern that may be a medical issue, please see a qualified healthcare professional.
Radia runs on modern iPhone and Android phones with a front camera, available from the Apple App Store and Google Play. Any recent device with a decent camera will work well. All you really need is one clear selfie in good light.
You can cancel anytime from your app store subscription settings (Apple App Store or Google Play). Cancelling stops future renewals, and you keep your Radia Plus access until the end of the current paid period. If you cancel during a free trial before it ends, you will not be charged.
Radia includes in-app account deletion. From your account settings, you can delete your account, which removes your account record, your photos, and your skin profile from our active systems. You can also email privacy@radia.app if you need help with a data request.
We are happy to help. Email us at support@radia.app or use our contact page. We aim to respond within two business days.
